About CurtRidge Solutions

Who We Are

CurtRidge Solutions LLC is a contractor-first, operations-focused company built on a foundation of hard work, integrity, and results. We serve industrial facilities across Oregon with reliable, on-the-ground services in plant operations, compliance, and safety. With decades of combined field experience, we understand the real-world challenges that plant managers, EHS professionals, and maintenance leaders face — because we’ve lived them.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality operations and maintenance services that ensure our clients' facilities run safely, efficiently, and in full compliance with all applicable regulations. We aim to support industrial progress while maintaining the highest levels of operational integrity and environmental responsibility. CurtRidge is committed to being the team you can count on when it matters most.

What Sets Us Apart

We don’t just meet the standard — we define it. Our experience in gasification, waste-to-energy, hazardous waste handling, and plasma-enhanced systems allows us to provide solutions that most contractors simply can’t. We thrive in complex, regulated environments and specialize in handling projects that require precision, discipline, and dependable follow-through. At CurtRidge, we take pride in building strong partnerships with our clients through transparency, loyalty, and results that speak for themselves.

Leadership That Works

Our leadership team is composed of industry veterans who’ve walked the floor, responded to critical system failures, led shutdowns, and managed crews through major infrastructure upgrades. We don’t lead from conference rooms — we lead from the field. CurtRidge Solutions is built for people who expect more from their contractors. We’re here to deliver.
